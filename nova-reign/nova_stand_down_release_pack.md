## STORY BEHIND THE TRACK (For Follow-Up Post)

> People ask me why I make music instead of just streaming or posting photos.
>
> Here's why: I was a Heavy Equipment Operator in the Marines. MOS 1345. You don't get that job because you're delicate. You get it because you can handle the weight.
>
> But the weight I brought home from service? That one took years to figure out.
>
> The tinnitus started while I was still in. The sleep issues came after. The hypervigilance — always scanning, always on — that one's still a work in progress.
>
> I found binaural beats by accident at 2am when nothing else was working. And something shifted.
>
> So I learned to make them myself. "Stand Down" is the first one I'm sharing publicly. It's not fancy. It's just honest.
>
> If it helps one veteran sleep tonight, that's the whole mission. 🎖️
>
> Charlie approved it too. (He fell asleep during the recording session. Good enough for me.)
