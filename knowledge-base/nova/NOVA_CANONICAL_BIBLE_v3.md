2. **REVIEW** — View every image, listen to every audio, watch every video frame-by-frame
3. **COMPARE** — Side-by-side comparison against `nova_canonical_reference_v3.jpg`
4. **AUDIT** — Run through all 10 checklist items above
5. **VERDICT** — PASS (publish) or FAIL (regenerate immediately)
6. **REPORT** — Provide clear pass/fail report with details of what was checked

- If ANY check fails → **DO NOT PUBLISH**
- Immediately regenerate the failed content
- Re-run the full audit on the regenerated content
- Repeat until ALL checks pass with zero anomalies
- Only then approve for delivery/publication
